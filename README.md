## Understanding UPSC CSE: The Road to IAS

The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) conducts the CSE annually to recruit for elite services like IAS, IPS, and IFS. It's a three-stage marathon: Prelims (objective, June), Mains (descriptive, September-October), and Interview (March-April). With over 10 lakh applicants vying for 1,000 seats, competition is fierce, but success rates hover around 0.1%—a testament to the exam's rigour.

Vacancies are limited, but the rewards immense: shaping policy, serving the nation, and job security. In Bangalore, where tech dreams often clash with public service aspirations, UPSC coaching bridges that gap. Choosing a good institute isn't just about classes—it's about holistic support that sustains you till selection.
